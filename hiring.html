Job Positions

01.

Cook

The Cook will be responsible for preparing and cooking menu items according to recipes and standards, ensuring the highest quality of food and presentation. The ideal candidate will have previous experience in a kitchen environment and strong culinary skills in creating delicious Vietnamese dishes.

02.

Server

Servers play a crucial role in creating a positive dining experience for our guests by providing excellent service and attention to detail. The ideal candidate will have strong communication skills and deliver exceptional hospitality.

03.

Busser

Busser plays a crucial role in ensuring a positive dining experience for our guests by maintaining cleanliness and organization in the dining area and assisting the serving staff as needed. The ideal candidate will have strong attention to detail, a positive attitude, and a willingness to work collaboratively as part of a team.

04.

Cashier

The Cashier plays a key role in providing excellent customer service by processing transactions accurately and efficiently, handling payments, and assisting customers with inquiries. The ideal candidate will have strong numerical skills, attention to detail, and a positive attitude towards serving customers.
